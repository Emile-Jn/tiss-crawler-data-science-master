        <h2>Learning outcomes</h2>

        <div class="encode"><p>After successful completion of the course, students are able to</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>precisely formulate essential analytical statements</li> 
 <li>prove theorems of real analysis</li>

    <h2>Subject of course</h2>

    <div class="encode"><p>Elementary set theory, systems of numbers, sequences and series of real numbers, functions of one real variable, continuity, differential calculus in one variable</p>
